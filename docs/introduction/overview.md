# Overview

## Admin
- Manages all platform operations, including school profiles, class offerings, and user accounts.
- Oversees scheduling, payments, instructor assignments, and student enrollment.
- Has access to analytics, reporting, and communication tools for efficiency and support.

## Teacher (Instructor)
- Delivers practical and theory lessons to assigned students.
- Manages personal availability, student lesson plans, and progress tracking.
- Communicates directly with students for feedback, scheduling, and lesson updates.

## Student
- Finds and connects with driving schools and instructors through the platform.
- Books and manages lessons, tracks learning progress, and accesses learning resources.
- Communicates with teachers and receives reminders, confirmations, and important updates digitally.
