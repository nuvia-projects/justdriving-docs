# Features

The financial management module equips Just Driving with a comprehensive, integrated set of finance-focused features to handle every aspect of school and student finances.

## Key features

- **Invoice management**  
  - Create, send, update, and track invoices for lesson packages, single lessons, fees, and materials.
  - Draft, sent, paid, partially paid, overdue, and cancelled statuses.
  - Support for custom line items and adjustments (discounts, surcharges).

- **Payment recording and reconciliation**  
  - Link student payments to specific invoices and automatically update invoice and balance status.
  - Support both online card payments (via payment gateways) and offline/manual methods (cash, bank transfer, MobilePay).
  - Record partial payments, overpayments, or multi-invoice settlements.

- **Automated reminders and notifications**  
  - Send reminders for upcoming due dates or overdue invoices via email and optional SMS.
  - Notify students and/or staff of payment receipts and key financial events.

- **Financial dashboards and reports**  
  - Overviews of open invoices, paid amounts, overdue balances, and revenue per period.
  - Filters for quickly locating students with outstanding debt or unusual account activity.
  - Export capabilities for accounting or auditing.

- **Integration with payment gateways**  
  - Secure online payment options for students integrated directly into the student portal.
  - Automated updates when payment gateways confirm (or fail to confirm) payments.
  - Support for provider-specific requirements (idempotency, receipts, transaction references).

- **Accounting and export features**  
  - Generate export files for external accounting systems or support API-based synchronization.
  - Map invoice items to appropriate accounting categories or codes per schoolâ€™s configuration.
  - Visibility and audit trails for all export and sync activity.

## Advanced and configurable aspects

- **Adjustment and correction tools**  
  - Staff can apply credits, issue refunds, or correct misapplied payments with full audit trail.
  - Support for voiding or cancelling invoices when services are not delivered.

- **Per-school finance configuration**  
  - Customize payment methods, invoice behavior, and automated flows to match unique school requirements.
  - Set taxes, surcharges, or pricing structures on a per-school basis.

- **Role-based finance access**  
  - Limit financial data access to only authorized staff and roles.
  - Teachers see only the information needed for teaching; admins manage full financials.

- **Compliance and transparency tools**  
  - Full reporting and export support for audit, compliance, and regulatory needs.
  - History and traceability for every invoice, payment, reminder, and configuration change.

These features ensure the finance system is both robust for daily operation and flexible enough for the various business models of different schools within Just Driving
